  • Full URL: https://www.youtube.com/embed/dTRKCXC0JFg?start=8&end=13&autoplay=1

  • Video URL: https://www.youtube.com/embed/dTRKCXC0JFg
    • used to get the embedded video that would normally be used for Iframes and such. Pretty much just the video without the youtube UI.

  • Query Parameters (everything after "?")
    • start=8 https://www.youtube.com/embed/dTRKCXC0JFg?start=8
      • Tells the request to begin serving the video 8 seconds in.
    • end=13 https://www.youtube.com/embed/dTRKCXC0JFg?start=8&end=13
      • Tells the request to only send up to the 13th second fo the video.
    • autoplay=1 https://www.youtube.com/embed/dTRKCXC0JFg?start=8&end=13&autoplay=1
      • Tells the request to automatically start playing the video when the page is loaded. By deafult the video will not begin playing on its own.
Wikipedia link that contains a fragment.

A fragment is just a reference to the html's own tree structure. Navigating to this will simply move the browser window into view of the referenced content.

  • Full URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Princess_Bride_(film)#Cast

  • Article URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Princess_Bride_(film)

  • Fragment "#Cast" https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Princess_Bride_(film)#Cast
    • This will move the window so that the html element with the id="Cast" will be at the top of the viewport.
